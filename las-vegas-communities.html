Las Vegas Communities

Summerlin

One of the nation's most celebrated master-planned communities, Summerlin spans 22,500 acres along the western edge of Las Vegas, offering over 200 miles of trails, world-class golf, top-rated schools, and beautifully designed villages.

View Homes
The Lakes

An established oasis in the heart of Las Vegas, The Lakes features a stunning 320-acre man-made lake surrounded by mature tree-lined streets, custom homes, and a welcoming country club lifestyle steps from the Strip.

View Homes
Southern Highlands

A prestigious master-planned community in the southernmost reaches of Las Vegas, Southern Highlands combines guard-gated security, a private golf club, resort-style amenities, and stunning Red Rock mountain views.

View Homes
Skye Canyon

A vibrant newer master-planned community in northwest Las Vegas, Skye Canyon offers modern homes, a resident clubhouse, parks, sports courts, and sweeping Spring Mountain and Red Rock views in a family-forward environment.

View Homes
Centennial Hills

Nestled in northwest Las Vegas near the Spring Mountains, Centennial Hills is a growing community known for its scenic desert backdrop, excellent schools, spacious homes, and easy access to both the valley and Red Rock Canyon.

View Homes
Mountain's Edge

One of the fastest-selling master-planned communities in Las Vegas history, Mountain's Edge in the southwest valley offers diverse home styles, over 500 acres of parks, scenic desert washes, and a strong community character.

View Homes
Desert Shores

A hidden gem in northwest Las Vegas, Desert Shores features four interconnected lagoons surrounded by custom homes, lush greenbelts, and walking paths — offering a rare waterfront lifestyle in the middle of the Mojave desert.

View Homes
Queensridge

A guard-gated luxury enclave adjacent to the prestigious Las Vegas Country Club, Queensridge delivers commanding Strip and valley views, elegant custom estates, and an exclusive address in the heart of western Las Vegas.

View Homes
Spanish Trail

An iconic guard-gated golf community in western Las Vegas, Spanish Trail wraps around a private 27-hole championship course, featuring custom estates, lush landscaping, and a timeless exclusivity just minutes from the Strip.
