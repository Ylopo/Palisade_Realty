Usage Rules
Gold (#b89a5e) — CTAs, eyebrows, stat numbers, accent bars, hover states, icon borders, badges, footer email
Gold Hover (#cbb278) — Button/link hover state only. Never used at rest.
Black (#000000) — Section headings on light backgrounds, footer, stats section background
Near Black (#212121) — Body text, FAQ text, blog card titles
Off White (#f5f5f5) — Section backgrounds for About, Blog. Creates visual rhythm.
White (#ffffff) — Button text on gold, text on dark sections, nav links, hero heading
Light Gray (#b8b8b8) — Stat labels, property detail text, footer nav links, captions
Dark Sections — #0d0d0d for Testimonial and Team sections, not pure black
